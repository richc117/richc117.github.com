¡Hello...

...and welcome to my website! My name is Richard Caballero. I'm a User Experience Researcher and Interaction Designer. I studied Cognitive Science and Human Computer Interaction at the University of California at San Diego. I love watching people and learning how our minds work. I spent the last year working as project manager at the Interactive Cognition Lab headed by Professor David Kirsh. During my time there I worked on several projects that involved Theory of Interaction, Environment Design, and Information Architecture.
