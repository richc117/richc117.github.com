¡Hello...

...and welcome to my website! I'm a User Experience Researcher and an aspiring Interaction Designer. I love this world and I'm tired of seeing people struggle to work with the technology that is suppose to simplify our everyday lives. I want to design never before seen interactive products that support and enhance the way we interact with each other and the environment we inhabit.

This last year I have been working as project manager at the Interactive Cognition Lab headed by Professor David Kirsh. During my time there I worked on several projects that involved Theory of Interaction, Environment Design, and Information Architecture.

On my spare time I like to dabble with web design and ruby. If I'm not geeking out on the web then I'll either be watching some sci-fi flick or reading a sci-fi novel about far off distant worlds being colonized by man.
